What areas do you cover?

I mainly cover NW10, NW2, and NW6 (Willesden, Kensal Green, Harlesden, Queen’s Park, Brondesbury, Kilburn, Cricklewood). If you’re just outside these areas, send your postcode and I’ll confirm availability.

How much do you charge?

Small jobs are priced at £50 call-out plus £50 per hour. Larger multi-step projects are quoted in writing once the scope is confirmed.

Full details are on the pricing and booking page.

What should I send for a fast quote?

Send your postcode, a short description, and 2–3 clear photos (plus any model numbers). WhatsApp is usually the quickest for photo quotes.

What are your working hours?

Standard hours are Mon–Sat 08:00–18:00. Out-of-hours emergency call-outs may be available — call or text to check.

Are you Part P registered?

I’m not registered with a Part P competent person scheme, so I can’t self-certify notifiable domestic work. If a job is notifiable under Building Regulations (Part P), I can coordinate with a registered electrician so the correct compliance route and paperwork are handled properly.

If you’re unsure, send details first and I’ll explain the options before any work begins.

References: IET Part P FAQ, GOV.UK Approved Document P, Planning Portal Part P.

How does certification work if my job is notifiable?

For notifiable domestic work, the usual options are:

  • A registered competent person electrician self-certifies under their scheme.
  • A registered third-party certifier inspects/tests the work.
  • Building Control is used as the route for sign-off.

We’ll confirm the right route before work starts.

References: IET certification routes, GOV.UK third-party certification schemes.

Who will attend — you or a partner electrician?

Most small jobs are carried out by David. If I’m fully booked or your job needs a registered electrician for certification, I can offer trusted local cover from my electrician network.

You’ll always know who is attending before anything is booked, and you can choose to wait for me or use the partner.

Will you share my details with anyone?

Only if needed to deliver your job — for example, if a registered electrician or trusted partner electrician is attending or certifying. I only share the minimum necessary details (such as name, contact details, address/postcode, and job notes) and I never sell your information.

See the privacy notice for details.

Reference: ICO Data sharing code of practice.

Do you offer emergency call-outs?

Sometimes. Out-of-hours emergency visits depend on availability. Call or text with your postcode and what’s happening and I’ll confirm options.
