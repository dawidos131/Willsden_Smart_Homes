    Common jobs in Willesden homes and shops

    Typical small-job scope in NW10, with practical timings:

    • Socket replacements and loose outlets: replace worn or heat-damaged accessories and re-terminate conductors safely; usually around 30-90 minutes per point depending on access.
    • Lighting faults and fitting swaps: resolve flicker, failed switches, or non-working fittings; many like-for-like swaps are completed in one visit.
    • Targeted fault finding for trips or dead points: isolate likely causes before replacing parts; typically 1-2 hours for a focused diagnostic visit.
    • Extractor fan and bathroom/kitchen controls: replace failed fan units or isolators to restore airflow and reliability; commonly 1-2 hours.
    • Smart thermostat installs and handover: fit and configure customer-supplied controls, then walk through app setup and scheduling; often completed in a half-day slot.
    • Video doorbell and camera power tidy-ups: install on existing feeds, improve reliability, and keep cable routes neat; timing depends on route/access complexity.
    • Small shop front/backroom fixes: switches, lights, fused spurs, and nuisance faults that affect day-to-day operation; planned to minimise disruption to opening hours.

    Pricing stays transparent: £50 call-out + £50/hour for small jobs, with quotes available for larger project scopes.

    Willesden FAQ

    Do you cover all of Willesden (NW2)?

    Yes. Willesden is inside the main NW2 coverage zone. If you are just outside NW2, send your postcode and I can confirm availability before booking.

    How quickly can small jobs be booked?

    Booking speed depends on the current diary, but clear photos and postcode details usually help you get a faster yes/no and timeslot options.

    What should I send on WhatsApp for a fast quote?

    Send your postcode, a short description of the issue, 2-3 clear photos, and any model numbers if devices are already purchased.

    Can you work in Willesden shops as well as homes?

    Yes, for small practical jobs on existing circuits and controls. Share access times so the visit can be planned around opening hours where possible.

    Do I get certificates for electrical work?

    Small non-notifiable jobs are tested and explained clearly. If your project is notifiable under Part P, a registered electrician can be arranged so certification is issued correctly.

    What about parking and access in Willesden?

    If parking restrictions or permit bays apply, include that in your message. Sharing access details upfront helps avoid delays and keeps the visit efficient.
