Privacy notice

This page explains what information is collected when you contact Willesden Smart Homes and how it is used.

Plain summary

Collected Contact details, postcode, job notes, and any photos you send.

Used for Quotes, booking coordination, and practical job delivery.

Shared only when needed For trusted delivery or certification support.

What information is collected

  • Name and preferred contact details you provide.
  • Postcode and job details needed to assess your request.
  • Photos or files you choose to send for quote purposes.

How your information is used

  • To reply to your quote or booking enquiry.
  • To plan visits, confirm scope, and send follow-up messages about your job.
  • To keep basic records of completed or pending work.

Information is not sold to third parties.

Storage and sharing

Information is stored in email and/or phone systems used to respond to enquiries. Where forms are configured, submissions may also be stored by the form provider used.

Information is not sold to third parties.

To deliver your quote or job, information may be shared when necessary — for example with a registered electrician (for notifiable work under Building Regulations Part P), or with a trusted partner electrician from my local roster (if they are attending on my behalf or providing overflow cover).

I only share the minimum needed to deliver your request, such as your name, contact details, job address/postcode, and a short job description/photos.

If a partner electrician attends, they may be a separate business and will use your details only for the purpose of carrying out the job you requested.

Further reading: ICO Data sharing code of practice and ICO guidance on contracts and data sharing.
